Getting Around Sydney

  • Train tickets from Sydney airport are expensive. ($18 each) It's cheaper to take an Uber from the Priority Pick-Up lot.
  • Uber is available but Ola is less expensive. They frequently have discounted rates and specials.
  • To take the Bus or Train you'll need an Opal card. We have a couple extras if you'd like to use them.
  • Ferrys. From Circular Quay you can take the ferry to a number of places. We've done day trips to Manly, Tarongo Zoo and Watson's Bay.
  • Traveling from the Eastern Suburbs (Bondi)? The easiest way to get to the city centre is by the 333 Bus.
